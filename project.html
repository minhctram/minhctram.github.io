Connecticut Explored

Connecticut Explored is a nonprofit that has a magazine, website, and podcast about the history of Connecticut. They needed an app for their platform, in order to reach a greater audience and have a gamified experience. The application CT Explored Guided Tours allows users to go on different tours across Connecticut and learn about Connecticut's history, based on what CT Explored posts on their website. They can save these tours for later and get directions on how to get there. Users can also earn points by posting on Instagram the different locations they visited, with a specific hashtag.

Product Owner: CT Explored

Technologies: React Native, Supabase, Google/Apple Maps, Expo

Platforms: Android, Apple
